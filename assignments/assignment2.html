Spaghetti Carbonara

A 5 ingredient dish with big flavor.

Tools Needed

  • Large Pot
  • Large Pan
  • Whisk
  • Medium Mixing Bowl
  • Colander

Ingredients

  • Dry:
    • Spaghetti, 220 grams
    • Guanciale, 120 grams (ok to use bacon)
    • Pecorino Romano, 30 grams
    • Black Pepper, to taste
  • Wet:
    • Olive Oil, 2 teaspoons
    • Egg, 1 large
    • Egg Yolk, 3

Steps to make:

This dish comes together quickly so have all of the ingredients prepped and ready to go before beginning for best results.

Guanciale should be cut to small pieces, about 3/16".

Combine the egg, 3 egg yolks, Black Pepper, and grated Pecorino Romano in a bowl and whisk together.

  1. Bring a pot of well salted water to boil.
  2. Boil the Spaghetti according to the package directions, typically about 8-9 minutes.
  3. Reserve about 1/2 cup of the pasta water
  4. While pasta is boiling, saute the guanciale in the olive oil until it is browned. If bacon is used, drain most of the grease from the pan once complete.
  5. When the pasta is cooked, drain it and toss it together with the guanciale.
  6. Slowly whisk the reserved pasta water into the egg mixture to temper the eggs. This will help avoid creating scrambled eggs in the pasta.
  7. Mix the pasta and egg mixture together and toss until the pasta is well coated.
  8. Serve immediately with additional pecorino romano and/or black pepper added if desired.

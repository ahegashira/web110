About Me

My name is Hiro Egashira and while I believe that a formal education can help people make progress in their careers, I do not believe that it is the only way to learn and I do not believe that a lack of formal education should limit anyone's options.

I created this collection of resources with that in mind and hope that it will help people interested in coding get their start on their journey. If you have any questions, comments, concerns, or just want to say hi then reach out with the form below.

Thank you for your support.

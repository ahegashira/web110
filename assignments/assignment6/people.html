Industry People

There are a lot of people in the programming world we will never hear about but there are some that have made themselves public figures in some way. This list is a small sample of people who I have followed in my short time learning that have made some impact on my learning process.

David Heinemeier Hansson

David Heinemeier Hansson (born 15 October 1979; also known to the Ruby programming language and automobile racing communities as DHH) is a Danish programmer and the creator of the popular Ruby on Rails web development framework and the Instiki wiki. He is also a partner at the web-based software development firm Basecamp.

Wes Bos

Wes Bos is a full stack web developer and designer from Hamilton, Canada. He loves to share what he knows through his training products, teaching, and speaking at conferences. Wes has been creating on the web for around 9 years and has become pretty good at it. He hangs out on Twitter and would love to meet you!

Sarah Drasner

Sarah Drasner is an award-winning Speaker, Head of Developer Experience at Netlify, Vue core team member, and Staff Writer at CSS-Tricks. Sarah is formerly Principal Lead of Emerging Markets, Cloud Advocates at Microsoft and Manager of UX & Engineering at Trulia/Zillow Group. She’s the author of SVG Animations from O’Reilly and has given Frontend Masters workshops. Sarah is a co-organizer of ConcatenateConf, a free conference for Nigerian and Kenyan developers. Sarah is also the co-founder of Web Animation Workshops with Val Head. She has worked for 15 years as a web developer, and at points worked as a Scientific Illustrator and a Professor in the Greek Islands.
